# The Economics of Epidemics

Welcome to the **Economics of Epidemics** project. This site showcases a collection of research, code, and educational materials developed by the Hunter College Economics faculty and students.

These materials were originally developed and presented at the **Roosevelt House Public Policy Institute at Hunter College** during the early days of the COVID-19 epidemic to help model and understand the unfolding crisis.

Our goal is to explore the intersection of epidemiology and economics, using mathematical models to understand disease dynamics and the impact of various policy interventions.
